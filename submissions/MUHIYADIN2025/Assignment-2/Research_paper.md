Research Paper: Banking Loan Approval and Risk Analytics DatasetTitle & Collection MethodTitle: Credit Risk Assessment and Financial Loan Approval DatasetCollection Method: This dataset was compiled from historical retail banking credit ledgers. It tracks demographic attributes, employment profiles, income levels, and credit histories for various loan applicants. The data acts as a financial ledger used to evaluate creditworthiness and automate credit risk management decisions during a financial evaluation cycle.Description of Features & LabelsFeatures Graduate).Self_Employed: Categorical (Employment type indicator: Yes for entrepreneurs/freelancers, No for salaried employees).ApplicantIncome: Continuous Numeric (The primary monthly/annual income of the main applicant).CoapplicantIncome: Continuous Numeric (The secondary income generated by a co-signer or partner).LoanAmount: Continuous Numeric (The total principal sum of money requested by the applicant).Loan_Amount_Term: Discrete Numeric (The agreed repayment duration period expressed in days/months).Credit_History: Binary Categorical (Historical credit behavior guidelines: 1.0 indicates a prime repayment history, 0.0 indicates a record of defaults or no history).Property_Area: Categorical (Geographic location classification of the collateral property: Urban, Semiurban, Rural).Label (y):Loan_Status: Binary Categorical (Target variable indicating final credit approval: Y for Approved, N for Rejected).This presents a classic Binary Classification task (predicting whether an account will clear risk filters or default).Dataset StructureRows: Multiple financial applicant profiles (samples).Columns: 13 variables (12 architectural features + 1 target classification label).Sample
